• High Concept: Two players need to cooperate to land a falling aircraft.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will cooperate and have fun struggling with awkward controls collaboratively.
  • Genre:Falling object simulator.
  • Game play: Player 1 controls the rotation of the craft, player 2 controls the pitch.
  • Prototype goal: To experiment with players cooperating to control a single entity.
  • Features: Features (will feature) a unique airplane-inspired control system.
  • Setting/Premise: A pilot and co-pilot have one arm each immobilized when their plane runs into a goose.
  • Story: This game doesn't have an overarching story.
  • Target Audience: People who want to play a collaborative game with a friend.
  • Play time: About 1 minute.
  • Strategies: More talking between players will help them coordinate movement.
  • Prototype Assets:I made all of them with the Gimp by hand.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: It's similar to Top Gun on the NES.
