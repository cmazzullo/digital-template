• High Concept: Play a police office who gives parking tickets while avoiding angry drivers.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will experience the stress of being parking enforcement officer while dealing with a surfeit of resources.
  • Genre:Falling object simulator
  • Game play: The player gives tickets to the falling cars and avoids the angry people.
  • Prototype goal: To experiment with a surfeit of resources as a game mechanics.
  • Features: Features cars with different sprites for ticketed and unticketed states.
  • Setting/Premise: You are a cop giving out parking tickets when you're attacked by angry car owners.
  • Story: This game doesn't have an overarching theory.
  • Target Audience: People who want to play a quick and fast-paced game.
  • Play time: About 3 minutes.
  • Strategies: Don't fall too far down or you'll be trapped under cars.
  • Prototype Assets:I made all of them with the Gimp by hand.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game:It's like a lot of other people's games, many of which featured falling objects.
