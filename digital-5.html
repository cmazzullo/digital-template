• High Concept: Two players compete to navigate a maze using gravity.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will have fun trying to compete with asymmetrical starting conditions.
  • Genre:Two player maze.
  • Game play: One player controls a phone-stealing demon, the other player chases it, trying to retrieve their phone.
  • Prototype goal: To make a solid functional game with two players.
  • Features: Features a complex map and two-player same-screen action.
  • Setting/Premise: A demon has stolen your cellphone. Alternatively, you are a demon who has just stolen a cell phone.
  • Story: This game doesn't have an overarching story, but it is suggestive. Satanic technochratic cultists? It bears further development.
  • Target Audience: People who want to play a collaborative game with a friend.
  • Play time: About 3 minutes.
  • Strategies: There unfortunately isn't too much in the way of strategies or meaninful decisions as far as I can see.
  • Prototype Assets:They are all from OpenGameArt.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: It's similar to the toy where you have a marble in a wooden maze and you have to navigate through it by tilting the box. The physical game gives you much more fine-grained control, though, which is a problem with this game.
