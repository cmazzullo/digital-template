• High Concept: Mine asteroids to collect minerals.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players will be able to explore different upgrade mechanics in a game with a satisfying core mechanics.
  • Genre:Asteroids derivative.
  • Game play:A player flies around mining asteroids and collecting their minerals.
  • Prototype goal: I wanted to put together a game focused on resource collection.
  • Features: Features explosions, triggered sound effects, different game states and music.
  • Setting/Premise: You are an asteroid miner who is farming an especially dangerous field.
  • Story: There is no further story.
  • Target Audience: Players who enjoy experimenting with upgrade systems.
  • Play time: Five minutes or so, maybe longer when more levels have been added.
  • Strategies: There unfortunately aren't much in the way of strategies or meaninful decisions as far as I can see.
  • Prototype Assets:All from opengameart.com.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: It's a lot like Asteroids.
